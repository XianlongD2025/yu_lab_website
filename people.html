Meet the team.

PRINCIPAL INVESTIGATOR
Guocan Yu
Tenure-track Associate Professor, Department of Chemistry
Visiting Professor, School of Medicine, Tsinghua University

The Yu Lab develops supramolecular and lipid-based nanomaterials for the precise in vivo delivery of RNA and other therapeutics. Our work bridges synthetic chemistry, materials engineering, and translational medicine — with a focus on organ- and cell-selective targeting, immunotherapy, and metabolic disease.
