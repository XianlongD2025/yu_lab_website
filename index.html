TSINGHUA UNIVERSITY — DEPARTMENT OF CHEMISTRY

Design the carrier.
Direct the delivery.
Transform the therapy.

We design lipid nanoparticles and functional nanocarriers that route RNA and other therapeutics to the right organ, the right cell, and the right intracellular destination. Our approach treats delivery as a design problem rather than an afterthought, working toward therapies that are safer and more precise.

NANOMEDICINE & IN VIVO DELIVERY

Research directions

CHEMISTRY → BIOLOGY → MEDICINE
01 / MATERIALS
Lipid nanoparticles & RNA therapeutics
Ionizable lipids, helper lipids and hybrid nanomaterials for efficient, safe delivery of mRNA and other nucleic acids.
02 / TARGETING
Organ- and cell-selective delivery
Design principles that route nanomedicines to selected organs, tissues and cell populations while reducing off-target exposure.
03 / THERAPY
Nanomedicine for immunotherapy
Delivery technologies that reprogram immune and metabolic pathways in vivo for vaccines, cancer and disease intervention.
